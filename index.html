Team Structure

Badger Solar Racing is made up of more than 60 undergraduate, and graduate students. To keep our work on track and organized we are split into many sub-teams, under three main categories.

  • Electrical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Electrical team is responsible for all hardware communication. From designing PCBs and wiring the car, to designing the dashboard and embedded code.

    • Sub-Teams

      Battery, High Voltage, Low Voltage, Solar Array, Software, Firmware

    • Director

      Jacob Hogan

  • Mechanical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Mechanical team is in charge of actually designing and assembling the car itself. From The brakes, to the shell, to testing it constantly.

    • Sub-Teams

      Body, Braking, Chassis, Ergonomics, Manufacturing, Mechatronics, Suspension

    • Director

      Jay Yoo

  • Operations

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Operations team handles all the day to day of Badger Solar Racing, from sponsoring the team and recruiting new members, to securing sponsorships and managing finances.

    • Sub-Teams

      Communications, Finance and Supply Chain, Industry Relations, Outreach and Recruiting, Virtual Reality, Vehicle Operations and Logistics, Website

    • Director

      Ryan Mirwald
