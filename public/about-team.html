Our Team

Badgerloop is comprised of three teams that come together to build the best pod possible.

  • Electrical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Electrial team is responsible for all hardware communication. From designing PCBs and wiring the pod, to desiging the dashboard and embedded code.

    • Sub-Teams

      Battery, Controls, Low Voltage, High Voltage, Software, Testing and Safety

    • Director

  • Mechanical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Mechanical team is in charge of actually designing and assebling the pod itself. From The brakes, to the shell, to testing it constantly.

    • Sub-Teams

      Analysis, Braking, Fabrication, Propulsion, Stability, Structural

    • Director

  • Operations

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Operations team handles all the day to day of Badgerloop, from sponsoring the team and recruiting new members, to securing sponsorships and managing finances.

    • Sub-Teams

      Communications, Feasibility, Finance and Supply Chain, Industry Relations, Outreach and Recruiting, Virtual Reality, Website

    • Director
