Team Structure

Badgerloop is made up of more thanategories.

Learn More

  • Electrical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Electrical team is responsible for hardware communication - from designing PCBs and wiring the pod, to designing the dashboard and embedded code.

    • Sub-Teams

      Battery, Controls, Low Voltage, Electrical Reliability, Software, Powertrain

    • Director

  • Mechanical

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Mechanical team is responsible for pod design and assembly - from the brakes, to the shell.

    • Sub-Teams

      Analysis, Braking, Mechanical Reliability, Propulsion, Stability, Structural

    • Director

  • Operations

    • Team Responsibilities

      The Operations team is responsible for recruitment and general management - from gathering new members and sponsors, to allocating finances.

    • Sub-Teams

      Communications, Feasibility, Finance and Supply Chain, Industry Relations, Outreach and Recruiting, Virtual Reality, Website

    • Director

About

We're a UW-Madison student organization whose goal is to design, build, test, and execute a pod in the SpaceX Hyperloop Competition.

And what drives us is the betterment of society, as well as the betterment of our skillsets.

But all of this wouldn't be possible if it weren't for our generous donors, who we can't thank enough!
