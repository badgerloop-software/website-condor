Pod IV Reveal

Posted on: April 4, 2019

On April 17th, 2019, we revealed our fourth hyperloop pod. Pod IV will compete in the 2019 SpaceX Hyperloop Competition later this summer!

“Thank you to all of our sponsors and supporters who joined us for the unveiling of our newest hyperloop pod. We have worked on this one for the past eight months, so we are very excited to show it off.” ~ Mark Swartz | Badgerloop President
