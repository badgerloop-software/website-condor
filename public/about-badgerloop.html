About Badgerloop

We are a registered student organization at the University of Wisconsinâ€“Madison that competes in the American Solar Challenge.

Made up of undergraduate and graduate students of various disciplines, any students who are interested in contributing to a new mode of transportation can join!

Team Goals:

Build the best solar car possible with our available resources

Develop the skills needed for high-level, professionalwork through hands-on experience gained in an engineering startup envirnoment

Learn from our mistakes and utilize Hyperloop Competition experience while paving new paths of understanding

Complete a minimum viable product for solar car to compete in the Summer 2022 Formula Sun Grand Prix
